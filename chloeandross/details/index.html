Order of the day

When to join us

12 noon

The ceremony

St. Comgall’s Church

Please arrive before 12 and aim to be seated by 11:45am. The bride arrives at 12 noon and the ceremony begins at 12.

After the ceremony

The reception

Manson’s Cottage

Once the ceremony is over, make your way to the house. The reception will be ready for you whenever you arrive.

12 midnight

The last dance

Home time

The DJ finishes at 12 midnight, so please have your journey home planned in advance.

Getting there

Make your own way to the house

There won’t be a bus between the church and the house, so please arrange your own transport. The good news is there’s plenty of parking at the house.

Reception address 10 Murdocks Lane
BT21 0FH

Look out for the Ross & Chloe’s Wedding sign and follow it if you’re not sure where to go.

Getting home

Please pre-book your taxi

Taxis can be very difficult to get on a Saturday night. We strongly recommend booking yours in advance—if you wait until midnight to phone one, you’ll likely struggle to get a lift home.

The bar

Drinks are on us

The bar is free, with a selection of beers, wine and spirits. If there’s something super specific that’s your one and only, feel free to bring it along.

A little note

An adults-only day

As much as we adore your little ones, our wedding will be a grown-ups-only celebration. We hope this gives you the chance to relax and enjoy the day with us.
